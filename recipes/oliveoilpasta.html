Olive Oil Pasta

Description

Olive oil pasta is one of the easiest dishes out there. Ever feel tired after work but want an amazing meal? This is the dish for you! In only 10 minutes, create one of the most famous dishes of all time!

Ingredients

  • spaghetti noodles
  • pine nuts
  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Begin cooking your noodles in boiling water.
  2. While the noodles cook, start chopping up some garlic.
  3. Add chopped garlic and pine nuts into a pan of 2tbsp of hot olive oil.
  4. Quickly add cooked noodles and some of your pasta water to the pan.
  5. Sir it all up and serve into bowls!
