Iced Coffee

Description

This delicious maple iced coffee will hit the spot on a hot summer day. Enjoy the best parts of coffee with a refreshing cool blast. Ditch the hot stuff! Get your favorite bag of beans out and lets get brewing!

Ingredients

  • coffee beans
  • almond milk
  • maple sirup
  • brown sugar (optional)
  • ice cubes

Steps

  1. Brew your coffee of choice-- make it as strong as possible.
  2. Transfer coffee into your favorite glass until it is around half-full.
  3. Add the maple sirup (and optionally brown sugar) while it is still hot & stir.
  4. Add milk to your liking, but leave enough room for ice.
  5. Add enough ice to cool the drink down!
