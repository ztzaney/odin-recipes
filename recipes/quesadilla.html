Cheese Quesadilla

Description

This versatile dish is great for a cook in a pinch. All your really need are a few core ingredients and you're ready to get started! Grab some tortillas and cheese and get creative with it!

Ingredients

  • tortillas
  • melty cheese
  • olive oil (for the pan)
  • beans (optional)
  • green veggies (optional)

Steps

  1. Get a pan hot with some oil to cover the bottom.
  2. Grab a tortilla and lay it in the pan.
  3. Begin adding cheese and any other fillings you would like.
  4. Once you have everything added, fold the tortilla closed and cook until all cheese is melted.
  5. Take the quesadilla out of the pan, slice it up, and chow!
